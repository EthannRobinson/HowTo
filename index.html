Efficient Crossfit Movements and Techniques

Clean and Jerk

Equipment

  • barbell
  • bumper plates
  • clamps

Steps

  1. Address the barbell with feet shoulder width apart
  2. Grab the barbell bending the knees while still keeping the back straight
  3. You need to stand up out of the squating position
  4. Once the bar is at you mid thigh you need to pop your hips to accelerate the barbell to the front rack positiom
  5. From the front rack position you dip your legs slightly then thrust the barbell overhead and lock your arms
